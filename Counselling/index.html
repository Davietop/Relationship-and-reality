Welcome to Relationship & Reality. Kindly feel very secure to seek help in whatever way you might be in need. We are here to help you scale through all difficulties to a point of getting desired results.

Finally, how can we be of help?

Individual counseling

Relationship issues might get so tough at times and the guidance of a professional might be needed to take the next "right" move. This category belong to those who want to receive counseling and guidance solely (alone). This process involves just one person with the counselor.

Premarital Counseling

Marriages are sweet especially when we open the photo book. The cake,the dress,the dance,the happiness and joy is such a nice thing to view but do you know that is just the "wedding ceremony" and not the "Marriage" itself? If you don't prepare well for your marital journey,you will end up being disappointed after the wedding ceremony. You will need the help of a professional relationship therapist to help you through the process of preparing and learning(this involves you and your partner). Marriage comes with a lot of challenges but there are ways to win over them. Premarital counseling is a luxury you should take advantage of. A great home won't happen by magic.

Mentorship

It is not that you are bad at making decisions but most of the times,the lack of a MENTOR in your life might be the huge gap between you and the success in want in your life. Having a mentor in your life can make a huge difference in your life. I am currently running mentorship programs for individuals who are willing.
